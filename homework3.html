Maggi

Maggi is an international brand of seasonings, instant soups, and noodles that originated in Switzerland in late 19th century.

  • Maggi – One packet
  • Water – A cup and a half
  1. Take one and a half cup of water in a pan.🍳
  2. Heat the pan on medium flame.🔥
  3. When the water comes to boil, add the Noodles Cake to the pan.
  4. Cover it with a lid for a minute.🕐
  5. After a minute, uncover the lid and add the tastemaker to the pan.
  6. Mix it well, Without breaking the Noodles.🥄
  7. Switch off the flame when all the Water boils.👨‍🍳
  8. Enjoy the hot Maggi in this lovely weather! 🍜
